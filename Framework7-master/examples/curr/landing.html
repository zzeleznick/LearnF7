HOW IT WORKS

Currency Hero is a peer to peer currency exchange platform specifically designed for overseas travelers. It is easy to use and there are only three steps to complete the exchange.

1. ENTER YOUR INFO

Specify the amount of currency you need. Set location for transaction if needed. Then click the button to find your "Hero"

2. FIND YOUR "HERO"

If you are buying foreign currency, just wait for your "Hero"s to show up with the currency you need! If you are selling foreign currency, be a "Hero" and find the person waiting for you!

3. VERIFY

Verify your "Hero" with the secret code you get. Hand the foreign currency and confirm the safe transaction on our app. After both confirmed the dollar amount will be paid to the "Hero".
